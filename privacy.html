We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through the website. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Children's Privacy

Gaming World is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this website, please contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.
