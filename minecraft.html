Start Your Minecraft Adventure

Minecraft gives players the freedom to explore large worlds, collect resources and build almost anything they can imagine. Every new world offers different landscapes, caves, villages and challenges.

In Survival Mode, players must gather food, craft tools, build shelters and protect themselves from dangerous creatures. Creative Mode provides unlimited resources and allows players to focus completely on construction and design.

Minecraft can also be played online with friends. Players can join multiplayer servers, complete adventures, build cities and enjoy custom game modes together.
