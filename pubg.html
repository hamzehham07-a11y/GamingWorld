Beginner Guide

PUBG Mobile is a competitive battle royale game where survival, strategy and teamwork are essential for victory.

Choose your landing location wisely, collect equipment quickly, communicate with teammates and always stay inside the safe zone.

Practice recoil control, use cover effectively and improve your aim to increase your chances of winning every match.
