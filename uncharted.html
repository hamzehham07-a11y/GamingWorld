Begin Your Uncharted Adventure

Uncharted takes players on dangerous journeys across jungles, mountains, cities and ancient ruins. Each adventure combines exploration, climbing, combat and puzzle-solving.

Nathan Drake uses his athletic skills to climb walls, cross dangerous gaps and escape collapsing environments. Players also use stealth and weapons when facing hostile enemies.

The series is best known for its cinematic presentation, humorous dialogue and exciting stories inspired by legendary treasures and historical mysteries.
