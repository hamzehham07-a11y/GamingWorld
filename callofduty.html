Call of Duty Beginner Guide

Call of Duty is a fast-paced shooter that rewards quick reactions, accurate aiming and smart movement.

Start by learning the maps and choosing weapons that match your preferred play style. Assault rifles are balanced, while sniper rifles are better for long-distance combat.

Always use cover, watch the minimap and communicate with your team. Completing objectives is often more important than only getting kills.
