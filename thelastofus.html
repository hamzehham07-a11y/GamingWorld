The Last of Us

The Last of Us is a story-driven action-adventure game set in a dangerous post-apocalyptic world filled with infected creatures and hostile survivors. Follow Joel and Ellie through an emotional journey where survival, trust and difficult choices shape every part of their adventure.

Survive The Last of Us

The Last of Us places players in a world changed by a devastating infection. Supplies are limited, and every area can contain infected creatures or dangerous human enemies.

Players must search rooms for ammunition, healing materials and crafting resources. Stealth is often important because direct combat can quickly use valuable supplies.

The relationship between Joel and Ellie is central to the experience. Their journey combines tense survival gameplay with one of gaming's most memorable emotional stories.
