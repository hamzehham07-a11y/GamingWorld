The Last of Us

The Last of Us is a story-driven action-adventure game set in a dangerous post-apocalyptic world. It follows Joel and Ellie as they travel across the United States.

About the Game

The game focuses on survival, emotional storytelling, exploration, and the relationship between its main characters. Players must survive against infected creatures and dangerous human enemies.

Gameplay

Gameplay combines stealth, shooting, exploration, resource collection, crafting, and close-range combat. Ammunition and supplies are limited, so players must use them carefully.

Main Characters

Joel is a survivor tasked with protecting Ellie, a young girl who may be important to humanity's future. Their journey becomes one of the most emotional stories in gaming.
