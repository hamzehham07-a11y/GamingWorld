Enter the World of Resident Evil

Resident Evil challenges players to explore dangerous locations such as abandoned mansions, police stations, villages and secret laboratories.

Ammunition and healing items can be limited, so players must carefully decide when to fight, escape or save resources for stronger enemies.

The series combines horror, mystery and action. Players solve puzzles, discover documents and uncover the truth behind biological outbreaks and secret organizations.
