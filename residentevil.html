Resident Evil

Resident Evil is one of the most famous survival horror game series, combining terrifying enemies, dark environments, action, exploration, and puzzles.

About the Game

The Resident Evil series follows different characters as they investigate dangerous outbreaks, secret laboratories, biological weapons, and mysterious organizations.

Gameplay

Players explore dangerous locations, collect weapons and healing items, solve puzzles, manage limited resources, and fight zombies and other terrifying creatures.

Survival Horror

Resident Evil creates tension through dark environments, limited ammunition, powerful enemies, unexpected attacks, and challenging puzzles.
